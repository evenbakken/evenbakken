moh.
Visual Identity

The design is a classic and timeless expression with clean colors and minimalistic elements.

'moh.' is a fictional restaurant which stands for 'meters above sea level' in norwegian. The consept is based on height which the identity demonstrates through the logo and other design elements.

Personalized logo for each restaurant with their own meters above sea level number and topographic rings in the amount of each 100 meters.
